Founder Story

I built ThriveMom because I was tired of feeling like I was failing at things that "should be easy."

Written by Heidi·Founder, ADHD mom of 3

I'm not someone who struggled because I didn't try.

I'm an accountant.

I've built systems.

I've led teams.

I know how to be organized.

And still…

At home, everything felt harder than it should.

I would forget things that mattered.

Start tasks and not finish them.

Get overwhelmed by simple decisions.

Not all the time.

But enough to make me question myself.

And that disconnect messed with my confidence more than anything else.

And the worst part wasn't the chaos.

It was the story I started believing about myself.

"That I should be able to handle this."

"That other moms can do it… so why can't I?"

So I did what I always do.

I tried to fix it with systems.

Planners.

Apps.

Better routines.

More discipline.

None of it worked.

Not because I didn't follow through… but because those systems were never built for how my brain actually works.

That's when it clicked.

This isn't a discipline problem.

It's a support problem.

So I stopped trying to force myself into systems…

…and started building one that actually supports me in real life.

On the messy days.

On the overwhelming days.

On the days when even thinking feels like too much.

That became ThriveMom.app

Not another productivity app.

But a place where you can:

  • Clear the mental chaos without organizing it first
  • Get one small step when everything feels too big
  • Reset in seconds instead of spiraling
  • Feel supported without being judged

I didn't build this as an expert looking in.

I built it as a mom living it.

Because here's what I know now:

You're not failing.

You've just been trying to use tools that don't support your brain… or your reality.

And once you have the right kind of support?

Everything feels lighter.

👉 Try ThriveMom and see what support actually feels like.
