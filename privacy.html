Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

ThriveMom ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website thrivemom.app. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

By joining the waitlist, you consent to receive launch-related emails. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. We will never sell or share your email with third parties for marketing purposes.

Our website does not use cookies for tracking.

You have the right to:

Contact us at support@thrivemom.app to exercise these rights.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
